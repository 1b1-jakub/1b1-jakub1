Recipe #1: Spaghetti

  • Time: 60min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 100g
Tomato sauce 150g
Pasta 100g

Step 1: Bring the water to a boil and add the pasta. Meanwhile in other pot add sauce and meat and start.
Step 2: Cook spaghetti until Al Dente and after 10 minutes stop cooking sauce.
Step 3: Serve spaghetti

Recipe #2: Meat balls

  • Time: 40min
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Meat 250g
Salt and pepper 5g each

Step 1: Form the balls meat.
Step 2: Fry on the pan untill they will be brown.
Step 3: Serve meatballs with potato puree or with rice.

Recipe #3: French fries

  • Time: 30min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Potato 500g
Salt and pepper how much u like
Salt and pepper 5g each

Step 1: Cut the potatoes into French fries and start frying the oil in the pan.
Step 2: After 3 minutes put French fries into pan.
Step 3: After 10 minutes take out the fries and serve with meat.
